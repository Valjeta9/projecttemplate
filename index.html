Aqua Suites

Welcome to Aqua Suites, your perfect escape in Dhermi. Nestled along the stunning Albanian Riviera, our hotel is designed to be a haven of tranquility and luxury. Each of our modern rooms and suites is meticulously crafted to offer premium comfort and style, featuring elegant decor, plush furnishings, and breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Our dedicated staff is committed to ensuring that your stay is nothing short of memorable. From the moment you arrive, you'll experience exceptional service that caters to your every need, making you feel right at home.

Indulge your senses with our delicious dining options, where you can savor a variety of local and international cuisines, all prepared with the freshest ingredients. Whether you're enjoying a romantic dinner on our terrace or a casual meal by the pool, our culinary offerings are sure to delight.

For those looking to unwind, our spa provides a serene retreat, offering a range of rejuvenating treatments designed to relax both body and mind. Take a break from your busy schedule and indulge in our signature massages, facials, and wellness therapies.
